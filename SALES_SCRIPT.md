# SwarmOS — Sales Call Script

## Goal
Get them to want more meetings → position yourself as the solution.

---

## Structure
1. Build context
2. Understand their current situation
3. Expose the problem
4. Show your solution
5. Close

---

## 1. OPENING (30 sec)

> "Hey, thanks for taking the call — I'll keep this super simple.
> I just want to understand how you're currently getting customers, and if it makes sense I'll show you how we can help. Sound fair?"

---

## 2. DISCOVERY

> "How are you getting new customers today?"

> "How many meetings are you getting per week?"

> "Are you happy with that or do you want more?"

Dig deeper:

> "What's the biggest bottleneck right now?"

> "Have you tried outbound before?"

---

## 3. PROBLEM

Repeat back:

> "So basically, you want more consistent meetings, but [problem they said]."

Example:

> "You're getting some leads, but it's not predictable."

---

## 4. YOUR OFFER

> "Got it — so what we do is pretty simple. We handle the outbound for you and book meetings directly into your calendar."

Pause.

> "So instead of you chasing leads, the system brings them to you."

DO NOT explain AI in detail.

---

## 5. RESULT FRAME

> "Most clients use this to get an extra 5–20 meetings per month without hiring SDRs."

---

## 6. CLOSE

> "If we could do that for you, would that be valuable?"

Wait for: "yes"

> "Perfect — we're onboarding a few companies right now. It's $1,000/month for us to run everything for you."

Pause.

> "Do you want me to set this up for you?"

---

## Objection Handling

### "Too expensive"
> "Totally fair — but if this brings even 1–2 clients, it pays for itself, right?"

### "I need to think"
> "Of course — what specifically do you want to think about?"

### "Does it work?"
> "That's exactly why we start small and test — no long-term risk."

---

## Memorize This

1. How do you get customers?
2. How many meetings?
3. Want more?
4. We book meetings for you
5. It's $X/month
6. Want me to set it up?
